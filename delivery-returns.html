Delivery & Returns

Everything you need to know about shipping and returns.

Last updated: April 2026

Production Time

All Brodé pieces are made to order in our Beirut atelier. Standard production time is 4–6 weeks from the date of order confirmation. For bespoke or heavily customised orders, production may take up to 8 weeks. We will keep you informed throughout the process via WhatsApp.

Shipping

We ship worldwide. Once your order is ready, it will be carefully packaged and dispatched via an insured international courier.

  • Lebanon: 1–3 business days after dispatch.
  • GCC (UAE, KSA, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman): 3–5 business days after dispatch.
  • Europe & USA: 5–10 business days after dispatch.
  • Rest of the World: 7–14 business days after dispatch.

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout and vary by destination. Free shipping is offered on all orders above $1,500.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees upon arrival in the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our pricing. Brodé has no control over these charges and cannot predict their amount.

Returns Policy

Because each Brodé piece is uniquely made to order, we are unable to accept returns or exchanges under standard circumstances. We encourage you to review all product details carefully before placing your order.

  • Made-to-order and customised pieces are final sale and non-refundable.
  • If your order arrives damaged or with a manufacturing defect, please contact us within 48 hours of receipt with photographs.
  • In the case of a confirmed defect, we will offer a repair, replacement, or store credit at our discretion.

Alterations & Adjustments

We understand that fit is everything. If your piece requires minor alterations upon arrival, we recommend visiting a trusted local seamstress. For significant fit issues arising from our measurements, please contact us directly and we will do our best to assist.

Alterations, plus size adjustments, and colour changes are available upon request before production begins. Please discuss these at the time of ordering via WhatsApp.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or email. You can use this to track your shipment through our courier's website.

Contact Us

For any delivery or returns enquiries, please reach out to us via WhatsApp +961 3 303 429. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
